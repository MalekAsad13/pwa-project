Dr.Malek is a passionate professor at Philadelphia University, where he teaches web development. With a deep interest in programming and design, he focuses on equipping students with the skills needed to create dynamic, responsive websites. Malek's teaching approach combines theory with practical projects, allowing students to gain hands-on experience in building websites from scratch. Outside of the classroom, he is involved in various tech meetups and enjoys mentoring aspiring developers. Malek holds a master's degree in Web Development and has previously worked as a front-end developer before transitioning into academia. In his free time, he enjoys photography and exploring new web technologies.
