Dr. Ahmad is a skilled professor at Philadelphia University, specializing in visual programming. With a passion for making complex concepts accessible, he teaches students how to use visual languages and tools to create interactive software applications. His courses focus on the development of graphical user interfaces (GUIs) and multimedia programming, equipping students with the skills to design visually engaging and functional programs. Dr. Ahmad holds a Ph.D. in Visual Programming and has a background in both academia and the software industry. Outside of teaching, he enjoys experimenting with new programming tools, contributing to open-source projects, and mentoring students interested in pursuing careers in software development.
