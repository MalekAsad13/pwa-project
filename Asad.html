Dr. Asad is a dedicated professor at Philadelphia University, specializing in mobile computing. With extensive expertise in mobile app development and wireless technologies, he teaches students how to design and implement mobile solutions for real-world challenges. Outside of teaching, Dr. Asad enjoys exploring new mobile platforms, reading tech blogs, and mentoring students in their tech careers.
